↗

↗

WiFi-controlled autonomous rover with live SVGA video streaming and real-time asynchronous motor control under severe bandwidth constraints. Decoupled async architecture achieves <50 ms control latency independent of video framerate. Dynamic frame-skipping and thermal throttling adapt to 1–10 Mbps WiFi variability while maintaining ~1 hour runtime on 3S LiPo.
